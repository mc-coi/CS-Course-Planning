## Technical Tips (Game Jam Mindset)

```
What game jams teach us about rapid development:

1. SCOPE DOWN — The best jam games are tiny but complete.
   A 5-second game that's polished beats a 30-level game that's broken.

2. POLISH FIRST, FEATURES LAST —
   One mechanic that feels amazing > five mechanics that feel rough.

3. PLAYTEST EARLY —
   The first 10 minutes of outside playtesting reveals 80% of your bugs.

4. DOCUMENT EVERYTHING —
   A well-documented broken feature is better than an undocumented one.

5. SHIP IT —
   Done and shipped beats perfect and unfinished every time.
```
