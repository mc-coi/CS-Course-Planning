## Portfolio Reflection Prompts

Take your time with these. Write at least 2-3 sentences for each.

```
GROWTH:
1. What did you know about programming / game design before this course?
   What do you know now? Describe the growth.

2. What was the single hardest concept in this course for you?
   How did you eventually get it? Or: how are you still working on it?

3. What is the piece of work from this year you're most proud of?
   (It doesn't have to be the capstone — could be a specific script, mechanic, or design decision.)

LOOKING FORWARD:
4. What would you want to learn next? (Advanced Unity topics, Unreal Engine, game art, 
   sound design, mobile development, game jam participation...)

5. Is game development something you want to pursue further?
   What's your next step after this class?

ADVICE:
6. If a student starting this course next year asked you for one piece of advice,
   what would you tell them?
```
